pendulum_nonlinear_ode_test

pendulum_nonlinear_ode_test, an Octave code which calls pendulum_nonlinear_ode(), which sets up the ordinary differential equations (ODE) that represent the behavior of a nonlinear pendulum of length L under a gravitational force of strength G.
