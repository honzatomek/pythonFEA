incidence

incidence, a dataset directory which contains examples of incidence matrices associated with a directed graph.

Reference:

  1. John MacCormick,
    Nine Algorithms That Changed the Future: The Ingenious Ideas that Drive Today's Computers,
    Princeton University Press,
    ISBN-13: 978-0691158198.
  2. Cleve Moler,
    Experiments with Matlab,
    Chapter 7: Google PageRank
    https://www.mathworks.com/moler/exm/chapters/pagerank.pdf
  3. Timothy Sauer,
    Numerical Analysis,
    Pearson, 2006,
    ISBN: 0-321-26898-9,
    LC: QA297.S348.

Sample Files:

  • mac1_inc.txt, a 5x5 incidence matrix discussed by MacCormack.
  • mac2_inc.txt, a 16x16 incidence matrix discussed by MacCormack.
  • moler_inc.txt, a 6x6 incidence matrix discussed by Moler.
  • sauer_inc.txt, a 15x15 incidence matrix discussed by Sauer.
  • six_inc.txt, a 6x6 incidence matrix discussed by MacCormack.
