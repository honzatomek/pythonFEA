TIMELINES
Files Describing Timelines

TIMELINES is a data directory which contains examples of files describing timelines.

A timeline is a graphical display of dates or duratations or lifetimes arranged in chronological order to allow comparisons, or to show which people were contemporaries, or which events occurred in close sequence.
