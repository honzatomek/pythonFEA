triangle_symq_rule

triangle_symq_rule, a MATLAB code which returns symmetric quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 50, over the interior of an arbitrary triangle in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

The original source code, from which this library was developed, is available from the Courant Mathematics and Computing Laboratory, at https://www.cims.nyu.edu/cmcl/quadratures/quadratures.html ,
