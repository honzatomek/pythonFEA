hypersphere_test

hypersphere_test, a MATLAB code which calls hypersphere(), which carries out various operations for an M-dimensional hypersphere, including converting between Cartesian and spherical coordinates, stereographic projection, sampling the surface of the sphere, and computing the surface area and volume.
