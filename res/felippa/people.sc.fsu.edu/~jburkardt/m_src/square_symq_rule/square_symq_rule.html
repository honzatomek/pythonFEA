square_symq_rule

square_symq_rule, a MATLAB code which returns symmetric quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 20, over the interior of the symmetric square in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

The original source code, from which this library was developed, is available from the Courant Mathematics and Computing Laboratory, at https://www.cims.nyu.edu/cmcl/quadratures/quadratures.html ,
