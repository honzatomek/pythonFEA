triangle_wandzura_rule

triangle_wandzura_rule, a MATLAB code which can return any of six Wandzura rules for quadrature over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

There are six rules, which have polynomial degree of exactness of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30.
