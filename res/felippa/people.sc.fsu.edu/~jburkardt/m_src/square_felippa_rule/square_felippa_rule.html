square_felippa_rule

square_felippa_rule, a MATLAB code which generates the points and weights of a Felippa quadrature rule over the interior of a square in 2D.

Actually, the word "square" is meant to designate any quadrature region defined by: 

        A(1) <= X <= B(1)
        A(2) <= Y <= B(2)
