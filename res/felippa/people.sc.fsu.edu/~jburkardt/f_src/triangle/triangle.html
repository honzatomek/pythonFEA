triangle

triangle, a FORTRAN90 code which can compute properties of a triangle, including angles, area, centroid, circumcircle, edge lengths, incircle, orientation, orthocenter, and quality.

These properties include:

  • Angles;
  • Area;
  • Centroid;
  • Circumcircle (center and radius);
  • Edge lengths;
  • Incircle (center and radius);
  • Orientation;
  • Orthocenter;
  • Quality: ratio of incircle radius / circumcircle radius;
